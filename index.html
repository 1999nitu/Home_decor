The Importance of Home Décor eCommerce

The home furnishing market is expanding at a rate of knots. With décor and interior design trends changing each year, retailers must stay relevant and visible. And what’s better at providing strong visibility to retailers than an eCommerce platform? Be it an app or a web store.

The truth is that the home décor market has fragmented competition, meaning no one business can influence customers to stay loyal to them for long. This is good news for new businesses and startups with great décor design ideas and innovative products. Having a fully-featured and modern-style eCommerce platform in such a scenario is nothing but a game-changer.

A conveniently functional online store provides a wider customer reach, 24/7 availability, cross-selling and upselling opportunities, better customer insights, and streamlined business operations via one platform. Additionally, augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technologies have taken furniture and décor shopping to the next level.

Key Highlights of

Home Décor eCommerce Development

Profile Management System

You need three key profile panels for your multi-vendor home furnishing eCommerce platform. The User Panel allows customers to create their profiles and manage their shopping activities. The Admin Panel manages and controls inventory, profiles, analytics, marketing, order fulfillment, and delivery. The Vendor Panel helps businesses register as vendors and manage their listings, orders, promotions, and delivery-related activities.

Social Login/Signup

With the social login feature, you can allow easy login to your customers using their Google, Facebook, and X (formerly Twitter) profiles to register to your home furnishing online store. The reduced time in login encourages potential customers to get engaged with your platform. Besides, “Guest Signup” is also a great feature to provide more shopping convenience to your customers.

Product Catalog

A user-friendly showcase of various home décor and furniture items! Your cataloging system takes care of all the product listings, categories, individual descriptions, images, and other details. A recommended practice is to add high-quality images from all angles. Additionally, a 360-degree image or video also enhances user experience.

Convenient and Secure Checkout

Integration of secure and reliable payment gateways improves customer trust. As for the checkout system, embedding a single-page checkout is the most viable option to lower cart abandonment rates and make the user experience better. Additionally, the guest checkout feature helps customers make purchases without creating an account.

Search and Filter

Advanced search and filter feature allows customers to look for their desired home furnishing items, sorting the product list by color, material, size, price range, category, availability, customer ratings, brand, and special features like eco-friendly, warranty, and customization options, etc. The more specific the filter options, the smoother the customer experience is!

Reviews and Rating Options

A must-have feature in your décor and furniture online store! The reviews and rating feature must be simple to use. The system that allows vendors and businesses to easily respond to these reviews boosts customer trust and the vendor’s business value. Similarly, adding features that allow fellow users to upvote and flag others’ comments encourages user engagement.
